GenAI App Rationalization Advisor – Prototype

AI-powered solution for optimizing enterprise application portfolios

Executive Summary

This prototype demonstrates how structured portfolio data and generative AI can be used to guide strategic application rationalization decisions.

The GenAI App Rationalization Advisor is an AI-powered prototype designed to help enterprises optimize complex application portfolios. It analyzes cost, usage, technology stack, business criticality, and risk data to provide clear recommendations to retain, retire, modernize, or consolidate applications. The system generates a portfolio-level report with transparent reasoning to support strategic decision-making. By automating analysis, it reduces manual workload and accelerates transformation initiatives.

Overview

This prototype was inspired by my experience as an Application Portfolio Manager (APM) at GE Vernova, where I saw firsthand how challenging it can be for large enterprises to manage hundreds or even thousands of applications across teams, departments, and regions. Over time, portfolios often become bloated due to organic growth, mergers, and decentralized decision-making, making it difficult to maintain alignment with business needs.

Maintaining and operating these applications can consume up to 70–80% of IT budgets, leaving little room for innovation. Many applications are redundant, outdated, or carry compliance and security risks. Traditional portfolio rationalization efforts are manual, slow, and influenced by unclear ownership or politics.

The GenAI App Rationalization Advisor addresses these challenges by leveraging generative AI to analyze structured and semi-structured data such as cost, usage, technology stack, and risk. It generates actionable recommendations along with a portfolio-level report and transparent reasoning to support faster, more informed portfolio decisions.

Key features

  • AI-Driven Recommendations: Uses application metadata and business context to guide rationalization decisions.
  • Redundancy & Risk Detection: Identifies legacy systems, compliance risks, and technical redundancies.
  • Portfolio-Level Report: Summarizes recommendations and rationale for all applications.
  • Scalable Analysis: Handles portfolios of thousands of applications across complex environments.
  • Governed AI Prompts: Ensures transparency, traceability, and auditability of AI-driven decisions.

Target users

  • CIOs, Application Portfolio Managers, and Enterprise Architects
  • Application Owners
  • Finance and Procurement Teams
  • Security and Compliance Teams

Business value

  • Reduction: Identifies redundant or low-value applications to guide rationalization decisions.
  • Supports Transformation: Provides insights to help plan cloud migration, M&A integration, and digital transformation efforts.
  • Operational Efficiency: Reduces manual workload for IT teams.
  • Strategic Alignment: Improves transparency and decision-making across the organization.

Technology overview

The prototype integrates Generative AI with structured portfolio data to automate rationalization analysis and generate a portfolio-level report.

  • Stack: Python, GPT-3.5-turbo, Tkinter, CSV import
  • Inputs: Application inventory, usage data, cost, compliance risk, owner input
  • Outputs: Disposition recommendations with rationale and a portfolio-level report (PDF/CSV)

MVP scope

  • Upload application inventory and usage data via CSV for analysis
  • Generate AI-powered recommendations for retaining, retiring, modernizing, or consolidating applications
  • Export report containing recommendations with an explanation

Roadmap

  • Feedback Loops: Capture user validation on AI recommendations to improve trust and system learning
  • Governance Guardrails: Protect critical applications, require human approval, and show confidence scores to ensure compliance
  • Embedding Similarity & Semantic Search: Detect functionally similar applications even if names differ and prepare for CMDB-based semantic search
  • Build trust, ensure compliance, and unlock deeper insights for smarter rationalization
