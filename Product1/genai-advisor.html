GenAI App Rationalization Advisor

Stakeholder-specific recommendations for application portfolio decisions

Executive Summary

The GenAI App Rationalization Advisor is an AI-powered prototype designed to help enterprises optimize complex application portfolios. It analyzes cost, usage, technology stack, and risk data to provide clear recommendations to retain, retire, modernize, or consolidate applications. The tool generates stakeholder-specific reports with transparent reasoning to support strategic decision-making. By automating analysis, it reduces manual workload and accelerates transformation initiatives.

Overview

This prototype was inspired by my experience as an Application Portfolio Manager (APM) at GE Vernova, where I saw firsthand how challenging it can be for large enterprises to manage hundreds or even thousands of applications across teams, departments, and regions. Over time, portfolios often become bloated due to organic growth, mergers, and decentralized decision-making, making it difficult to maintain alignment with business needs.

Maintaining and operating these applications can consume up to 70–80% of IT budgets, leaving little room for innovation. Many applications are redundant, outdated, or carry compliance and security risks. Traditional portfolio rationalization efforts are manual, slow, and influenced by unclear ownership or politics.

The GenAI App Rationalization Advisor addresses these challenges by leveraging generative AI to analyze structured and semi-structured data such as cost, usage, technology stack, and risk. It generates actionable recommendations to retain, retire, modernize, or consolidate applications, along with stakeholder-specific reports and transparent reasoning to support faster, more informed portfolio decisions.

Key features

  • AI-Driven Recommendations: Uses application metadata and business context to guide rationalization decisions.
  • Redundancy & Risk Detection: Identifies legacy systems, compliance risks, and technical redundancies.
  • Stakeholder-Specific Reports: Tailored for CIOs, application owners, finance, and security teams.
  • Scalable Analysis: Handles portfolios of thousands of applications across complex environments.
  • Governed AI Prompts: Ensures transparency, traceability, and auditability of AI-driven decisions.

Target users

  • CIOs, Application Portfolio Managers, and Enterprise Architects
  • Application Owners
  • Finance and Procurement Teams
  • Security and Compliance Teams

Business value

  • Reduction: Identifies redundant or low-value applications.
  • Faster Transformation: Accelerates cloud migration, M&A integration, and digital transformation efforts.
  • Operational Efficiency: Reduces manual workload for IT teams.
  • Strategic Alignment: Improves transparency and decision-making across the organization.

Technology overview

The prototype integrates Generative AI with structured portfolio data to automate rationalization analysis and generate transparent, stakeholder-specific reports.

  • Stack: Python, GPT-4, Tkinter, CSV/CMDB import
  • Inputs: Application inventory, usage data, cost, compliance risk, owner input
  • Outputs: Disposition recommendations with rationale, stakeholder-specific reports (PDF/CSV)

MVP scope

  • Upload application data via CSV or form input
  • Generate disposition recommendations
  • Export stakeholder-specific reports

Roadmap

  • Clustering and grouping of applications
  • Slack alerts and notifications
  • ServiceNow export integration
  • Feedback loops for continuous improvement
