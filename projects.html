AMS Health Analyzer

Objective: Bring an externally purchased predictive maintenance tool into production to reduce equipment downtime and deliver customer value.

Role: Technical Product Manager / Project Lead

Actions:

  • Engaged stakeholders to define business requirements, clarify use cases, and establish success metrics.
  • Coordinated 6+ cross-functional teams including vendor, cloud, security, and enterprise architects.
  • Led implementation in AWS, including infrastructure provisioning, configuration, and installation.
  • Developed and delivered training materials to ensure adoption and proper use of the tool.

Results:

  • Tool fully integrated and operational; engineers detected equipment issues earlier, improving uptime and operational safety.
  • Set standard for integrating external tools and enabled potential new predictive maintenance services.
  • Avoided wasted software spend.

SQDC Highlights:

  • Safety: earlier detection of issues
  • Quality: proper governance and user training
  • Delivery: smooth cross-functional rollout
  • Cost: leveraged existing asset, avoided wasted software
