AI and Emerging Technology Prototypes

These prototypes represent a hands-on exploration of how Artificial Intelligence can enhance enterprise decision-making across domains. Each project builds on the lessons of the previous one, showing a clear progression in capability and complexity from structured data analysis to semantic search to full-stack predictive and generative AI.

The journey began with the GenAI App Rationalization Advisor, which introduced AI-driven insights to help organizations streamline their application portfolios. Building on that, the Semantic CMDB Search Tool explored how AI could make enterprise data more discoverable and accessible. The journey culminates in the AI Benefits Cost and Utilization Analyzer, a flagship prototype that unites predictive forecasting, computer vision, and generative reporting within a governed Data Lakehouse environment.

Together, these prototypes demonstrate how AI can be designed, governed, and scaled to deliver measurable business value across industries.

GenAI App Rationalization Advisor

AI-powered application portfolio analysis tool. I led the MVP design, workflow, and integration, while generative AI (ChatGPT) acted as developer, data scientist, and prompt engineer.

  • Generative AI (GPT-3.5/GPT-4) with prompt engineering
  • CSV & CMDB input, automated PDF/CSV reports
  • Tkinter GUI for interactive local prototype

Technology Overview

This section highlights the AI technologies and enterprise tools integrated across my prototypes, along with my role in leading their design, implementation, and governance. While I did not write production code, I managed technical architecture, MVP scope, and integration of AI technologies into business workflows. Generative AI (ChatGPT) acted as developer, data scientist, and prompt engineer under my guidance.

AI & Machine Learning

  • Generative AI: OpenAI GPT-3.5/GPT-4 for recommendations, insights, and conversational interfaces (prompt engineering included)
  • Semantic Search: Embeddings for natural-language search of complex datasets
  • Predictive Analytics (planned): Forecasting trends from structured and unstructured data
  • Computer Vision (planned): Extracting information from claims and invoice images

Data & Storage

  • Local Data Lakehouse: MinIO, Apache Spark, Dremio, Project Nessie
  • Vector Databases: Chroma for semantic search storage
  • CSV & CMDB Integration: Enterprise application inventory and metadata

Application & Interface

  • Web Interfaces: Streamlit for interactive dashboards and analysis tools
  • Desktop GUI: Tkinter for local applications
  • Backend Orchestration: FastAPI for workflow management and data processing

Data Sources & Inputs

  • Enterprise CMDB (ServiceNow PDI)
  • Application portfolios (cost, usage, risk, business metadata)
  • Employee benefits claims and utilization datasets (CMS, Medicare, BLS, HR systems)

Outputs & Deliverables

  • Actionable recommendations with transparent rationale
  • Stakeholder-specific reports (PDF, CSV)
  • Interactive dashboards for insights, exploration, and decision-making

Key Focus Areas in TPM Role

  • Defining MVP scope and prototyping roadmap
  • Coordinating AI implementation with ChatGPT (acting as engineer and data scientist)
  • Ensuring governed, secure, and scalable AI experiments
