AI & Product Innovation

I design and prototype AI-driven decision-support systems that address real enterprise challenges, particularly in areas where data is fragmented and decisions require context, validation, and accountability.

My work focuses on applying AI within structured workflows, using techniques such as retrieval-augmented generation and semantic search to surface relevant insights while maintaining transparency and control.

These prototypes are grounded in enterprise use cases, integrate with systems such as ServiceNow CMDB, and are designed to support measurable outcomes rather than experimentation alone. Examples include:

  • GenAI Application Rationalization Advisor – a decision-support tool that assists in evaluating retain, retire, and modernize decisions across an application portfolio.
  • Semantic CMDB Search Tool – a semantic search capability that transforms complex ServiceNow CMDB data into structured, actionable insights.
  • AI Benefits Cost & Utilization Analyzer (planned) – exploring predictive analytics and AI-assisted analysis to improve cost visibility and utilization insights.
